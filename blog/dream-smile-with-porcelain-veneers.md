### What are Porcelain Veneers?

**[Porcelain veneers](/porcelain-veneers)** are ultra-thin, custom-made shells of high-quality ceramic designed to cover the front surface of your teeth. Think of them like a protective, aesthetic “shield” that instantly masks flaws while mimicking the light-reflecting properties of natural tooth enamel.

At our Orange, NJ office, we use advanced materials to ensure your veneers are as durable as they are beautiful. In fact, we specialize in **minimal-prep veneers** that can be as thin as 0.3 mm—about the thickness of a contact lens!

### Why Choose Porcelain Veneers?

Veneers are one of the most versatile cosmetic treatments available because they can correct multiple issues at once:

-   **Permanent Whitening:** Ideal for deep stains or discoloration that professional bleaching can’t fix.
-   **Closing Gaps:** Easily eliminate unwanted spaces between your front teeth without the need for braces.
-   **Fixing Chips and Cracks:** Restore the smooth, uniform look of teeth worn down by age or minor accidents.
-   **Correcting Misalignment:** Often called “instant orthodontics,” veneers can make slightly crooked teeth appear perfectly straight in just two visits.
