# Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA)

**Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA)** is a sleep disorder where throat tissues collapse during sleep, blocking the airway and causing breathing pauses. This can lead to loud snoring, fatigue, morning headaches, and serious health risks like heart disease or high blood pressure.

At **Yogi Dental**, we screen for OSA and collaborate with sleep specialists for accurate diagnosis. For many patients, we offer **custom oral appliances**—a comfortable, non-invasive alternative to CPAP machines that keep the airway open for restful, uninterrupted sleep.

#### **Benefits include**:

-   Better sleep and reduced snoring

-   Increased energy and focus

-   Lower health risks

-   Comfortable, travel-friendly treatment

Let us help you breathe easier and sleep better naturally.
