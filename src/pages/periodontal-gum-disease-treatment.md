## Effective Gum Disease Treatment for a Healthier Smile

Protect your gums and teeth with our expert periodontal care. We treat gum disease with deep cleaning, scaling, and root planing to stop infection, prevent tooth loss, and restore oral health. Early treatment leads to better results—schedule your gum evaluation today!

### Signs and symptoms of periodontal disease:

1.Bleeding gums — Gums should never bleed, even when you brush vigorously or use dental floss. 2.Loose teeth — Also caused by bone loss or weakened periodontal fibers (fibers that support the tooth to the bone). 3.New spacing between teeth — Caused by bone loss. 4.Persistent bad breath — Caused by bacteria in the mouth. 5.Pus around the teeth and gums — A sign that there is an infection present. 6.Receding gums — Loss of gum tissue around a tooth. 7.Red and puffy gums — Gums should never be red or swollen. 8.Tenderness or Discomfort — From plaque or calculus.

### How Periodontal Gum Disease Treatment Works:

During your routine checkup, Yogi Dental will examine your gums for periodontal disease. A periodontal probe is used to evaluate if there is any breakdown in your gum tissue or if deep pockets have developed between your teeth and gums. Your Periodontal Gum Disease Treatment will depend on the type of periodontal disease you have and how far the condition has progressed. We offer advanced laser surgery procedures that eliminate pain, bleeding, swelling and discomfort.
