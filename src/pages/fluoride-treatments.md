## Protective Fluoride Treatments for Stronger, Healthier Teeth

Fluoride treatments help prevent cavities, strengthen enamel, and reduce tooth sensitivity. Ideal for kids and adults, this quick, painless service offers long-term protection against decay and keeps your smile healthy and bright. Ask us about adding fluoride to your next visit!

### How Fluoride Treatments Work

Fluoride is a natural mineral found in water and foods that helps protect teeth by strengthening enamel. It prevents decay by aiding remineralization—restoring lost minerals to teeth—and resisting acid attacks from plaque and sugar. In children under six, fluoride strengthens developing teeth, making them more resistant to future decay. For both kids and adults, it also reduces acid production and speeds up enamel repair, keeping smiles healthier and stronger.

### In What Forms Is Fluoride Available?

Fluoride is found in food, water, and dental products like toothpaste and rinses. At Yogi Dental, we offer high-strength fluoride treatments—gel, foam, or varnish—for stronger cavity protection. We also provide prescription fluoride supplements in liquid or tablet form for added care.
