##### About Us

## Comfortable, Natural-Looking Dentures to Restore Your Smile

Regain confidence with full or partial dentures designed for comfort, function, and a natural look. Our custom-fit solutions help restore chewing, speech, and your smile’s appearance. Yogi Dental ensures a secure, comfortable fit to improve your daily life and oral health.

### How Dentures Work

We offer complete, partial, and implant-supported dentures. Options include immediate or conventional types. Implant dentures provide better chewing, no palate, and a natural feel. Partials prevent shifting, and immediates ensure you never go without teeth during healing.

### What does getting dentures involve?

The process of getting dentures requires several appointments, usually over several weeks. Highly accurate impressions (molds) and measurements are taken and used to create your custom denture. Several “try-in” appointments may be necessary to ensure proper shape, color, and fit. At the final appointment, your dentist will precisely adjust and place the completed denture, ensuring a natural and comfortable fit.
