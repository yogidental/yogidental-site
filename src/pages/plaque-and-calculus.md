## Plaque and Calculus (Tartar)

**Plaque** is a sticky film of bacteria that forms daily on your teeth. If not removed, it hardens into **tartar (calculus)**, which can only be removed by a professional. This buildup can cause **gum disease, bad breath, tooth decay, and tooth loss**.

At **Yogi Dental**, we recommend professional cleanings every 6 months to remove tartar, protect your gums, and keep your smile healthy.

#### **Key benefits include:**

-   Preventing gum disease and tooth loss

-   Keeping breath fresh and teeth clean

-   Early detection of dental issues

-   Maintaining a bright, healthy smile
