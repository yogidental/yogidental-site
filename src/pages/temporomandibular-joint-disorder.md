# Temporomandibular Joint Disorder (TMJ/TMD)

The **temporomandibular joint (TMJ)** connects your jaw to your skull, enabling everyday actions like chewing and speaking. When this joint becomes strained or misaligned, it can lead to **TMJ disorders**, causing symptoms such as jaw pain, headaches, clicking sounds, earaches, and difficulty chewing.

We conduct a detailed evaluation checking jaw movement, bite alignment, and muscle tension—and offer treatments such as:

-   **Custom bite splints** to reduce clenching/grinding

-   **Physical therapy referrals** to restore jaw function

-   **Stress-reduction techniques** to ease tension

#### Benefits of TMJ Treatment:

-   Relieves jaw pain and headaches

-   Improves chewing and speaking comfort

-   Prevents further joint damage and tooth wear

-   Enhances overall jaw mobility and function

Early treatment can greatly improve quality of life , don’t ignore persistent jaw discomfort.
