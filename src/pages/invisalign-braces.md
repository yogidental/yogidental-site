## Straighten Your Smile Discreetly with Invisalign

1.  Straighten Your Smile with Invisalign
    Want a straighter smile without metal braces? Invisalign is a comfortable, lear alternative that fits your lifestyle. Using a series of
    nearly invisible, custom-made aligners, Invisalign gently moves your teeth into the right position–no wires, no brackets, and no
    food restrictions. You can remove the aligners to eat, brush, and floss, making it easy to keep your teeth clean. Most people won’t
    even notice you’re wearing them! With regular check-ins, you’ll see your smile transform in just a few months. Ready for a
    confident, beautiful smile? Ask us if lnvisalign is right for you!

### Invisalign Braces offer you many unique advantages over traditional metal braces:

Invisalign aligners are clear, comfortable, and removable—making them nearly invisible and easy to wear. They work faster than metal braces, cause less discomfort, and fit into your daily routine. Plus, they’re affordable with flexible payment options for every budget.

### How Invisalign Braces Work:

1,A series of clear plastic “aligners” are custom made to fit your teeth and mouth. 2.Each successive Invisalign brace is worn for 2-3 weeks all day and night, except 3.while eating and brushing. While wearing your Invisalign braces your teeth are gently moved to the desired position over time. 4.The duration of the process of aligning and straightening teeth varies, but is usually just 6-14 months.
