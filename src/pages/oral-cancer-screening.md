## Early Detection with Expert Oral Cancer Screenings

Protect your health with our quick, painless oral cancer screenings. We use advanced techniques to detect early signs of cancer, helping ensure timely treatment. Regular screenings offer peace of mind and are key to maintaining long-term oral and overall health.

### How Oral Cancer Screening Works

Oral cancer is one of the most curable diseases when it’s caught at early stages. That’s why the OralID oral cancer screening exam has been developed. An annual OralID exam, in combination with a regular visual examination, provides a comprehensive oral screening procedure for you. The OralID exam is fast and painless, and could help save your life. OralID uses technology that has proven successful in identifying soft tissue abnormalities in various areas of the body. A OralID exam is particularly important if you are at increased risk for developing oral cancer.

### OralID is performed immediately following a regular visual examination

1.First, you will be instructed to rinse with a cleansing solution. 2.Next, the overhead lighting will be dimmed. 3.Then, your dental professional will examine your mouth using OralID, a specially designed fluorescent light technology.
