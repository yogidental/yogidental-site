## Porcelain Bridges to Restore Function and Complete Your Smile

Porcelain bridges fill gaps left by missing teeth, restoring your smile and bite. They’re custom-made to match your natural teeth, offering a durable, aesthetic solution that prevents shifting and maintains facial structure. Get lasting function and beauty with porcelain bridges.

### Porcelain Fixed Bridges offer you many unique advantages…

1.Fill space due to missing teeth 2.Maintain facial shape 3.Prevent remaining teeth from drifting out of position 4.Restore chewing and speaking ability 5.Restore your smile 6.Upgrade from a removable partial denture to a permanent dental appliance

### How Porcelain Dental Bridges Work:

A dental bridge is a fixed, non-removable solution to replace missing teeth. Porcelain bridges are popular for their natural look and durability. They involve placing crowns on two anchoring teeth with artificial teeth in between. The process typically takes two visits, including impressions, a temporary bridge, and final fitting. With proper care and regular dental visits, your bridge can restore function and appearance for many years
