# Cracked Tooth

A cracked tooth can cause pain when biting or sensitivity to hot or cold. If untreated, it may lead to infection or even tooth loss. At **Yogi Dental**, we use advanced diagnostics like transillumination and CBCT to detect cracks early and treat them with crowns or root canals.

#### **Benefits of choosing Yogi Dental:**

-   Early detection prevents complications

-   Preserves your natural tooth

-   Relieves pain and sensitivity

-   Prevents infection and further damage

-   Restores full function and appearance of your smile
