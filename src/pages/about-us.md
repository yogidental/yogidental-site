**Meet Dr. Rajal J. Patel** – Experienced, Compassionate Care

Dr. Rajal J. Patel has been creating healthy, confident smiles since 1990. A proud graduate of New York University’s College of Dentistry, Dr. Patel brings decades of expertise to every patient she treats. With advanced training in orthodontics, Dr. Patel is certified in Invisalign clear aligners, nitrous oxide sedation, and all aspects of general dentistry. She collaborates closely with a trusted network of oral surgeons and periodontists, ensuring seamless, comprehensive care for even the most complex cases.

Outside the office, Dr. Patel is an avid reader, enjoys cooking, and is actively involved in local community service. Her dedication to lifelong learning and her warm, patient-centered approach make her a favorite among patients of all ages.

### Meet Dr. Niraj Patel, D.D.S.

<div className="yd-doctor__photo">

**Meet Dr. Niraj** – Your Smile Architect

Welcome! I’m Dr. Niraj, and if you’re looking for a dentist who blends artistry, science, and a dash of humor, you’ve come to the right place. I began my journey at New York University, earning both my undergraduate and dental degrees—go Violets! My passion for dental innovation led me to achieve Associate Fellowship status with the American Academy of Implant Dentistry in 2015. Since then, I’ve trained at leading institutions like the Spear and Zimmer Institutes, mastering everything from implant placement and bone grafting to sinus lifts, endodontics, and restorative design. Dentistry isn’t just about teeth—it’s about your whole well-being. I take a comprehensive approach, evaluating jaw joints, bite alignment, and the root causes of tooth wear. Whether I’m placing implants, crafting crowns, performing root canals, or perfecting smiles with Invisalign and veneers, every detail matters. I’m proud to work alongside Dr. Patel, combining our expertise to detect and treat cavities and gum disease early. Together, we’re committed to keeping your smile healthy and beautiful for years to come. I use advanced 3D imaging with Cone Beam CT technology—think of it as GPS for your mouth—to ensure precise diagnosis and treatment. The result? Comfortable care, lasting results, and a smile you’ll love to show off. Whether you’re here for a checkup or a complete smile makeover, I promise to make your experience as comfortable—and maybe even as enjoyable—as possible.
