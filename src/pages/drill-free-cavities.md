## Drill-Free, Gentle Cavity Fillings

Treat cavities without drills or numbing! The Fotona Laser offers a quiet, comfortable solution that removes decay gently and precisely—ideal for kids and adults alike.

### Gentle, Drill-Free Cavity Filings with the Fotona Laser

Gentle, Drill-Free Cavity Filings with the Fotona Laser Say goodbye to the dental drill! With the Fotona Laser, getting a cavity filled is now faster, quieter, and much more comfortable. Instead of using traditional tools, we use a gentle laser to remove the decay from your tooth. Most patients don't need a numbing shot, and there's no loud noise or uncomfortable vibration. The Fotona Laser is precise, So it only removes the damaged part of your tooth and keeps the healthy part safe. The laser also helps clean and sterilize the area, which means less risk of infection and faster healing. Most people can go right back to their day after their appointment. If you're looking for a stress-free, comfortable way to fix cavities, ask us about laser fillings at your next visit!
