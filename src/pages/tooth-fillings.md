## Tooth-Colored Fillings for a Strong, Natural-Looking Smile

Restore decayed or damaged teeth with durable, tooth-colored fillings that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth. Our fillings are safe, mercury-free, and provide long-lasting protection while preserving your smile’s appearance and strength. Comfortable and effective care at Yogi Dental.

### Do you suffer from a tooth that has decay, cracks or fractures?

Porcelain (Composite) fillings can be used to restore your damaged teeth, are non-toxic and can be matched to the color of your existing teeth. 1.Chipped teeth 2.Closing space between two teeth 3.Cracked or broken teeth 4.Decayed teeth 5.Worn teeth

### How Mercury-Free Tooth-Colored Porcelain Fillings Work:

Porcelain fillings repair teeth damaged by decay, cracks, or fractures. The affected area is cleaned and filled with durable, tooth-colored composite material, usually in one visit. If decay is near the nerve, protective medication is applied. Fillings are shaped and polished to restore tooth function and appearance. Mild sensitivity may occur but subsides quickly. Proper oral care and regular dental visits help maintain the filling’s longevity.
