## Natural-Looking Porcelain Crowns to Restore and Protect Your Teeth

Porcelain crowns restore damaged or decayed teeth with strength and a natural look. Custom-made to match your smile, they protect tooth structure, improve function, and enhance appearance. Yogi Dental ensures a perfect fit for long-lasting comfort and confidence.

### Porcelain Crowns offer you many unique advantages:

1.Restore Broken or Fractured Teeth 2.Improve Your Smile Through Cosmetic Enhancement 3.Removes Decay and Protects Teeth 4.Repairs Fractured Fillings 5.Protects Teeth that have Large Fillings 6.Protects Teeth that have had Root Canal (Endodontic) Therapy

### How Porcelain Crowns Work:

Porcelain crowns fully cover and restore damaged teeth to their original shape and strength. Ideal for teeth that can't be saved with fillings, they look natural and are highly durable. The process takes two visits: one for impressions and a temporary crown, and another to place your custom crown. Your dentist ensures a perfect fit, proper bite, and long-term comfort. With regular care, crowns offer lasting protection and a natural appearance for a confident smile.
